Peace of mind

Don't worry about your sump pump

If you own a home with a basement, you know that your sump pump is the first line of defense against flooding. Basements flood when sump pumps stop working - whether that's due to a power outage or an intense storm. SumpSense continuously monitors your sump pump - and if there's a problem, you'll find out right away.

Set it & forget it

Monitors your sump pump 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

SumpSense is easy to install and use. Simply plug it in and then drop the anchor into your sump pit. After you connect your mobile device to your SumpSense with our simple mobile app you're all set.
